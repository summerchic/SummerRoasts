Spicy Chicken and Vegetable Skewers(Roasted Chicken Char Cheat)

Quick Prep Recipes (Summer Roasters)

Step 1. Cut Chicken roaster in 4 and remove both breasts and cut in to squares and add 1 tbps olive oil to coat.

Step 2. Add 5 chicken pieces to skewers with your favorite vegetables

Step 3. Heat grill

Step 4. Add skewers to hot grill for 3 minutes for grill marks

Step 5. Add your favorite side dishes for a complete meal or enjoy.

Quick Tips:

Soak wooden skewers for 10 minutes to avoid burning on the grill.

During the summer months, less time in the kithen allows for more time outside.

Chicken Roasters can be bought at your local supermarkets or organic health food store for less that 10$ and can feed up to 4-6 people.
