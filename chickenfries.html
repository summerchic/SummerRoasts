Chicken with Baked Fries and Salad(Easy Oven Bake)

Quick Prep Recipes (Summer Roasters)

Step 1. Cut up roasted chicken in to 8 (2 Breasts, 2 Thighs, 2 Legs, 2 Wings)

Step 2. Cut up chicken breasts in to pieces. Add 1/2tbsp crushed garlic and a dash salt and pepper. Place in oven safe bowl with 4 tbsp watern.

Step 3. Peel and cut 2 potatoes and cut in to strips, season with 1/2tbsp pepper, rosmary, garlic powder and basil (you can always use frozen fries)

Step 4. Place fries in oven on 400 degrees for 40 minutes. Set oven timer for 30 minutes and then add chicken bowl to oven to heat up chicken pieces.

Step 5. Prepare a salad

Quick Tips:

Save roasted chicken drippings to use for a broth. Can be stored in fridge for 3 days.

During the summer months, less time in the kithen allows for more time outside.

Chicken Roasters can be bought at your local supermarkets or organic health food store for less that 10$ and can feed up to 4-6 people.
