Spicy Chicken with Tomatoes(Smothered Chicken)

Quick Prep Recipes (Summer Roasters)

Step 1. Using the remaining chicken thighs, wings and legs from the chicken roaster

Step 2. Cut up 1 tomato, 3 cloves of Garlic, half meduim onion (yellow or red) and 1/2 cup spinach

Step 3. Add 2 tbps oil to hot pan, add onions, garlic and tomato. Saute on medium heat for 7 minutes

Step 4. Add chicken pieces to pan with 2 tbsp of water or saved chicken broth. Cover and saute on low for 5 min

Step 5. Add your favorite side dishes for a complete meal.

Quick Tips:

Chicken bones can be used to also make chicken broth. Boil chicken bones for 45 min in salted water and let cool. Viola!

During the summer months, less time in the kithen allows for more time outside.

Chicken Roasters can be bought at your local supermarkets or organic health food store for less that 10$ and can feed up to 4-6 people.
